👤 About Me

Professional Summary

I am a digital development and innovation professional with extensive experience designing, managing, and scaling technology-enabled solutions across humanitarian and development contexts. My work spans public health, gender-based violence protection, supply chain, and social protection — delivered in close partnership with UN agencies, governments, international NGOs, and research institutions.


I bring a strong track record in end-to-end product lifecycle management — from human-centered discovery and requirements definition, through agile development and multi-country deployment, to training, handover, and sustainability planning. I am skilled at translating between technical and non-technical stakeholders, structuring complex requirements in ambiguous environments, and ensuring digital products are safe, contextually relevant, and truly user-focused.


My recent focus has been on emerging digital tools for protection and health — including Progressive Web Apps, mobile safe spaces, USSD/SMS systems, and AI-assisted care platforms — particularly in fragile, low-connectivity, and humanitarian settings across Sub-Saharan Africa, MENA, South Asia, and Latin America.

What I Do Best

Leading multi-country deployments in fragile and humanitarian contexts · Designing and managing USSD/SMS and mobile applications for client engagement · Turning data into actionable analytics and dashboards · Building and motivating cross-functional, cross-cultural teams · Stakeholder coordination across UN, governments, donors, and tech partners

What Drives Me

I am deeply motivated by the intersection of technology and human dignity. Whether supporting a survivor of gender-based violence to find services, enabling a nurse to register a patient, or helping a programme manager visualize data in real time — I believe thoughtfully designed digital tools can genuinely improve lives at scale.
